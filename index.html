LIVE IN PRODUCTION — SHIPPED FAMILY LEARNING PLATFORM

IGNITE — Family Knowledge, Activity & Rewards Hub

Another real, shipped product: IGNITE is a full learning-and-engagement platform built for families, applying the exact same "knowledge hub" thinking behind Hekima Hub — but for parents and kids instead of internal staff. It combines a structured content library of 100+ guided family activities with KidBucks, a native rewards currency kids earn by completing activities, redeemable through an integrated online gifting program. A built-in referral program turns every engaged family into a growth channel. It's the same core architecture I'd bring to Craft Silicon: a gated knowledge/activity library, a rewards-driven engagement loop, and a referral-led growth mechanism — proven here in a live consumer product, not just a concept.
